Jeon Soyeon:
Leader of a New Generation



Under Cube Entertainment, Soyeon (Korean: 소연; Japanese: ソヨン) is a South Korean rapper, singer-songwriter, and producer. The main rapper and leader of the girl group (G)I-DLE is her.

She goes by Jeon Soyeon in full when performing as a soloist. The single "Jelly" marked her solo debut on November 5, 2017.

Soyeon participated in the survival show Produce 101 and Unpretty Rapstar Season 3, ranked 20th and second respectively. She failed to debut with I.O.I. and signed an artist contract with Cube Entertainment on December 29, 2016.

Soyeon re-debuted as leader of (G)I-DLE in 2018, releasing her second single, "Idle Song," and becoming the fifth member on April 12. The group debuted on May 2 with their first mini album, I Am.
